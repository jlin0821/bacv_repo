Why someone should try

Someone should try out looking into linguistics so they can know more about languages. There are people who know nothing about the connections between communities in the past and languages can be a map to it. You should also try understanding linguistics as it deals with nature of language and communication and helps you understand context that you may not normally understand if you are an outsider of a language. It is an understudied field but offers a possible explanation to things. There is a greater depth in understanding a language rather than just memorizing phrases for communication.
